What is Full Stack Development?

Full stack developement means an engineer who can handle all the work of databases, servers, systems engineering, and clients. Depending on the project, what customers need may be a mobile stack, a Web stack, or a native application stack.

Why Full Stack?

Companies are looking at hiring developers who are experts in all facets of the development process. They are looking at people who can play different roles like a designer, developer, and database specialist. Specialist in single subsystems like only front-end or back end cannot understand the bigger picture of the entire system.

Career in Full Stack Developement

According to the Labor-Statistics-U.S. Bureau reports Full Stack Developer jobs will by 2024 exceed 853,000. Demand for this job role is especially high in product based companies, both in the startup sector and elsewhere. In fact, industry reports show that there has been a 20% growth in the demand for full-stack developers in 2018 v/s 2017 in INDIA.
